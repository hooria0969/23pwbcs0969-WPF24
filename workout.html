WORKOUTS

Cardio Workout

1. Running

Running helps make your heart stronger and burns lots of calories.

Routine

Run for 20 minutes, resting 2 minutes after every 5 minutes.

2. Jump Rope:

A fun exercise that works your whole body and burns calories quickly.

Routine

Run for 30 seconds, rest for 30 seconds. Repeat 10 times.

3. Burpees:

A full-body exercise that burns fat and improves fitness fast.

Routine

Do 10 burpees, rest for 1 minute. Repeat 3 times.

Strength Training Workouts

1. Squats

Great for building stronger legs and glutes.

Routine

Do 3 sets of 15 squats. Use weights if you want a challenge.

2. Push Ups:

Strengthens your chest, shoulders, and arms, while also working your core.

Routine

Do 3 sets of 12 push-ups. Do knee push-ups if regular ones are too hard.

3. Deadlifts:

This exercise builds strength in your back and legs.

Routine

Do 3 sets of 10 deadlifts with a comfortable weight.
