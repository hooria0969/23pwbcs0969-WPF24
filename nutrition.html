NUTRUTIONAL PLANS

Weight Loss Meal Plan

Breakfast

Oatmeal with Berries

Start your day with a warm bowl of oatmeal topped with fresh berries.

Benefits

Helps you stay full longer and keeps your blood sugar levels stable, perfect for weight loss.

Lunch

Grilled Chicken Salad

Enjoy a light salad made with grilled chicken and colorful, fresh vegetables.

Benefits

Supports your weight loss goals by providing important vitamins and minerals while keeping calories low.

Dinner

Steamed Veggies with Rice

Savor a healthy dinner of steamed vegetables served with Rice, a nutritious whole grain that adds protein.

Benefits:

Low in calories but full of nutrients, helping you feel satisfied without overeating.

Muscle Gain Meal Plan

Breakfast

Scrambled Eggs & Whole Wheat Toast

Start your day with fluffy scrambled eggs on whole wheat toast.

Benefits:

Keeps you full and provides nutrients your muscles need to grow.

Lunch

Rice, Chicken & Yogurt Bowl

Enjoy a nutritious bowl filled with quinoa, grilled chicken, with yogurt bowl.

Benefits:

Supports muscle growth and keeps your energy levels up throughout the day.

Dinner

Brown Rice with Salmon

Treat yourself to a plate of brown rice paired with grilled salmon, rich in healthy fats and protein.

Benefits:

Aids muscle repair and provides nutrients good for overall health.
